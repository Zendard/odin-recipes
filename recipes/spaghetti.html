Spaghetti

This is the spaghetti we will make

Spaghetti is a classic and the second thing people think of when thinking about italian food after pizza. So let's make it!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Green bell pepper
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Cook the meat with onion and bell pepper
  2. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and spices
  3. Cook and stir until ready
  4. Serve hot
